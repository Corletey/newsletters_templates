Connecting Our Team, Celebrating Our Work

Dear Team, thank you for your dedication to advancing African higher education. This month’s newsletter highlights our collective achievements, upcoming events, and ways to stay connected. Let’s keep the momentum going! – Your HR Team

Company News & Updates

The 16th AAU General Conference in Rabat was a resounding success! Our team’s efforts in organizing the event led to record attendance and impactful discussions. We also welcomed 10 new member universities, expanding our network across Africa.

Additionally, we’ve upgraded our office Wi-Fi to improve connectivity for all staff. Contact IT for setup assistance.

Employee Spotlight: Amina Mensah

Meet Amina Mensah, our Communications Officer, who led the social media campaign for the General Conference. Her creative posts reached over 10,000 views on Twitter! Outside work, Amina is passionate about photography and volunteers at a local youth mentorship program.
